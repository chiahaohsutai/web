MY STORY

I was born and raised in Montevideo, Uruguay, and I came to the United States to earn my bachelor's degree. Originally, I was planning to major as an industrial engineer. From the beginning, I wasn't very convinced. about the major and didn't know what I wanted to do in the future, so I decided to stick to the major and see what happens. This was a good and bad decision at the same time. I discovered that I was really passionate about programming even though I had no experience in coding; however, I did spend two years working towards a major I wasn't really passionate about.

Currently, I'm working towards a computer science and mathematics degree, and my dream is to become a software developer when I graduate.
